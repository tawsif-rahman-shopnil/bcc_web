About BAIUST Computer Club

The Bangladesh Army International University of Science and Technology (BAIUST) Computer Club is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting the study and understanding of computer science and technology among the students of BAIUST. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore their interests in technology, develop their technical skills, and network with like-minded individuals.







Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore their interests in technology, develop their technical skills, and network with like-minded individuals. The club was founded with the aim of creating a community of students who share a passion for computer science and technology. We host a variety of events and activities, such as coding workshops, tech talks, hackathons, and project competitions, to help students expand their knowledge and skills in this dynamic and ever-growing field.

Our Vision

In addition to our internal events and activities, we also participate in regional and national tech competitions and collaborate with industry partners to bring real-world experience and opportunities to our members.

At BAIUST Computer Club, we believe that technology has the power to transform the world, and we are dedicated to fostering the next generation of tech leaders and innovators. If you are a student at BAIUST and are passionate about computer science and technology, we invite you to join us and be a part of our community.
